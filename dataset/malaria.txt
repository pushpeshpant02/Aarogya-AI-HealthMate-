Overview:
Malaria is a mosquito-borne disease caused by Plasmodium parasites.

Common Symptoms:
- High fever, chills, and sweating
- Headaches and body aches
- Nausea and vomiting
- Fatigue

General Advice / Self-care:
- Seek medical care immediately if suspected.
- Rest and hydrate.
- Complete prescribed antimalarial treatment.

Prevention Tips:
- Sleep under insecticide-treated bed nets.
- Use repellents and wear protective clothing.
- Take prophylactic medicine when traveling to endemic areas.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Severe weakness, anemia, or confusion.
- Seizures or breathing difficulty.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
