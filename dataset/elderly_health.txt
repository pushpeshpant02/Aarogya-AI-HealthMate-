Overview:
Elderly health focuses on maintaining independence, preventing disease, and promoting quality of life in older adults.

Common Symptoms (issues):
- Memory loss or cognitive decline
- Frailty and risk of falls
- Vision and hearing problems

General Advice / Self-care:
- Engage in light physical activity daily.
- Eat a nutrient-rich diet with adequate calcium and vitamin D.
- Stay socially connected to avoid isolation.

Prevention Tips:
- Regular check-ups and screenings.
- Home modifications to prevent falls.
- Vaccinations (flu, pneumonia, shingles).

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Sudden confusion or memory loss.
- Unexplained weight loss or weakness.
- Falls or injuries.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
