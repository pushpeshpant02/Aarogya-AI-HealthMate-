Overview:
Child health focuses on growth, development, and prevention of diseases in children.

Common Symptoms (issues):
- Frequent infections
- Poor growth or weight gain
- Behavioral or developmental delays

General Advice / Self-care:
- Ensure proper nutrition including breastfeeding.
- Keep vaccinations up to date.
- Encourage outdoor play and activity.

Prevention Tips:
- Promote hygiene and healthy eating.
- Provide a safe home and school environment.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- High fever, breathing problems, or seizures.
- Signs of malnutrition or stunted growth.
- Developmental delays.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
