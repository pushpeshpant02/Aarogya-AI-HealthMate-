Overview:
The common cold is a mild viral infection of the nose and throat.

Common Symptoms:
- Runny or stuffy nose
- Sneezing and sore throat
- Mild cough
- Fatigue

General Advice / Self-care:
- Rest and stay hydrated.
- Use salt water gargle for sore throat.
- Use a humidifier to ease congestion.

Prevention Tips:
- Wash hands often.
- Avoid touching face with unwashed hands.
- Stay away from people who are sick.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Symptoms lasting more than 10 days.
- Severe sore throat, sinus pain, or high fever.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
