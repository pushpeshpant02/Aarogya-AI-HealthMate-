Overview:
COVID-19 is a respiratory illness caused by SARS-CoV-2, transmitted through respiratory droplets and aerosols.

Common Symptoms:
- Fever, cough, fatigue
- Loss of taste or smell
- Breathing difficulty
- Muscle aches

General Advice / Self-care:
- Rest and stay hydrated.
- Isolate to avoid infecting others.
- Use paracetamol for fever and aches.

Prevention Tips:
- Vaccination is key.
- Wear masks in crowded spaces.
- Wash hands frequently.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Difficulty breathing.
- Chest pain or pressure.
- Confusion or bluish lips/face.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
