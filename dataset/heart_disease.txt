Overview:
Heart disease refers to a range of conditions affecting the heart, including coronary artery disease and heart failure.

Common Symptoms:
- Chest pain or discomfort (angina)
- Shortness of breath
- Fatigue and dizziness
- Swelling in legs or ankles

General Advice / Self-care:
- Eat a heart-healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
- Exercise regularly under medical guidance.
- Take prescribed medications consistently.
- Monitor blood pressure and cholesterol.

Prevention Tips:
- Avoid smoking and limit alcohol intake.
- Maintain a healthy weight.
- Manage stress effectively.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Chest pain or pressure lasting more than a few minutes.
- Severe shortness of breath.
- Fainting or sudden dizziness.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
