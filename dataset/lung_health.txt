Overview:
Lung health is vital for breathing and oxygen supply, and can be affected by infections, smoking, or pollution.

Common Symptoms (issues):
- Chronic cough or wheezing
- Shortness of breath
- Chest pain

General Advice / Self-care:
- Avoid smoking and secondhand smoke.
- Stay physically active to improve lung capacity.
- Use protective masks in polluted environments.

Prevention Tips:
- Get vaccinated against flu and pneumonia.
- Maintain clean indoor air quality.
- Reduce exposure to allergens and pollutants.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Persistent cough lasting more than 3 weeks.
- Coughing up blood.
- Sudden or severe breathing difficulty.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
