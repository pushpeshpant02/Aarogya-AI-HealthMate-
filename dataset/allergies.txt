Overview:
Allergies occur when the immune system reacts to harmless substances like pollen, dust, or food.

Common Symptoms:
- Sneezing, runny nose
- Itchy, watery eyes
- Skin rashes or hives
- Wheezing or shortness of breath

General Advice / Self-care:
- Avoid known allergens.
- Use antihistamines for relief (if advised).
- Keep windows closed during high pollen seasons.

Prevention Tips:
- Regularly clean living spaces to reduce dust and mold.
- Wash bedding frequently.
- Use protective masks if needed.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Severe reactions with swelling of lips/tongue or breathing difficulty (anaphylaxis).
- Persistent symptoms interfering with daily life.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
