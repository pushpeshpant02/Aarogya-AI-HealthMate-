Overview:
HIV (Human Immunodeficiency Virus) weakens the immune system, and untreated can progress to AIDS.

Common Symptoms:
- Fever, night sweats
- Weight loss
- Frequent infections
- Swollen lymph nodes

General Advice / Self-care:
- Take antiretroviral therapy (ART) consistently if prescribed.
- Maintain a healthy diet and lifestyle.
- Protect against opportunistic infections.

Prevention Tips:
- Practice safe sex.
- Avoid sharing needles.
- Regular testing for at-risk individuals.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Unexplained weight loss or fever.
- Severe infections.
- If exposed to HIV (consider post-exposure prophylaxis).

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
