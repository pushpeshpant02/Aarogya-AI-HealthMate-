Overview:
Environmental health focuses on external factors affecting health, such as air, water, and housing.

Common Issues:
- Respiratory problems from pollution
- Waterborne diseases from unsafe water
- Skin conditions from poor sanitation

General Advice / Self-care:
- Drink safe, clean water.
- Use masks in polluted areas.
- Maintain hygiene at home and work.

Prevention Tips:
- Support policies reducing pollution and waste.
- Avoid burning plastics or harmful substances.
- Promote clean energy use.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Breathing issues worsening due to pollution.
- Persistent stomach illnesses from unsafe water.
- Severe skin infections.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
