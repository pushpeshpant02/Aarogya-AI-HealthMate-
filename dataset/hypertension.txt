Overview:
Hypertension (high blood pressure) is when blood pressure in the arteries is consistently too high, often without symptoms.

Common Symptoms:
- Usually asymptomatic
- Sometimes headaches, shortness of breath, or nosebleeds

General Advice / Self-care:
- Reduce salt intake.
- Exercise regularly.
- Manage stress with relaxation techniques.
- Avoid tobacco and limit alcohol.

Prevention Tips:
- Maintain a healthy weight.
- Eat a diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
- Regularly monitor blood pressure.
