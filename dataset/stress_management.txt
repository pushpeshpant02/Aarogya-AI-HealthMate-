Overview:
Stress management is key for mental and physical well-being.

Common Symptoms of Stress:
- Anxiety or irritability
- Sleep disturbances
- Headaches or muscle tension

General Advice / Self-care:
- Practice relaxation techniques (deep breathing, meditation).
- Exercise regularly.
- Maintain healthy sleep and diet patterns.

Prevention Tips:
- Plan and prioritize tasks to avoid overload.
- Maintain social support networks.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Severe anxiety or depression.
- Inability to cope with daily life.
- Suicidal thoughts.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
