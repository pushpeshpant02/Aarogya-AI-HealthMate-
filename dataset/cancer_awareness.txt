Overview:
Cancer is a group of diseases involving abnormal cell growth with the potential to spread.

Common Symptoms:
- Unexplained lumps or swelling
- Persistent fatigue or weight loss
- Changes in skin or moles
- Persistent pain or cough

General Advice / Self-care:
- Maintain a healthy diet and exercise.
- Avoid tobacco and limit alcohol.
- Attend regular screenings as advised.

Prevention Tips:
- HPV and Hepatitis B vaccination.
- Early detection through self-exams and medical screenings.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Any new or unexplained lump.
- Unusual bleeding.
- Persistent unexplained symptoms.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
