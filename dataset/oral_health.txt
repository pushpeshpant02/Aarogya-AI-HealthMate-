Overview:
Oral health is essential for overall health, covering teeth, gums, and mouth care.

Common Symptoms (issues):
- Toothache or cavities
- Bleeding gums
- Bad breath

General Advice / Self-care:
- Brush teeth twice daily with fluoride toothpaste.
- Floss daily to remove food particles.
- Limit sugary snacks and drinks.

Prevention Tips:
- Regular dental check-ups.
- Use protective gear (mouthguards) in sports.
- Avoid tobacco and limit alcohol.

- Persistent bleeding gums.
