Overview:
Hepatitis is inflammation of the liver, commonly caused by viral infections (A, B, C, D, E).

Common Symptoms:
- Fatigue and weakness
- Loss of appetite, nausea
- Jaundice (yellowing of skin/eyes)
- Abdominal pain

General Advice / Self-care:
- Avoid alcohol and maintain a healthy diet.
- Rest and hydrate.
- Follow antiviral treatments if prescribed.

Prevention Tips:
- Get vaccinated (for Hepatitis A and B).
- Avoid sharing needles or razors.
- Practice safe sex.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Severe jaundice or confusion.
- Abdominal swelling or pain.
- Bleeding tendencies.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
