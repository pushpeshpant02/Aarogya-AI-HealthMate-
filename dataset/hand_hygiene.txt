Overview:
Hand hygiene is the most effective way to prevent the spread of infections.

Common Symptoms (poor hygiene risks):
- Frequent colds or flu
- Stomach infections

General Advice / Self-care:
- Wash hands with soap for at least 20 seconds.
- Use alcohol-based sanitizer when soap is unavailable.
- Wash before eating and after bathroom use.

Prevention Tips:
- Educate children on proper hygiene.
- Clean and disinfect frequently touched surfaces.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Persistent infections despite hygiene.
- Severe gastrointestinal issues.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
