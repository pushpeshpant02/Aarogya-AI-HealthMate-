Overview:
Childbirth is the process of delivering a baby, requiring proper care and support.

Common Symptoms (labor signs):
- Regular contractions
- Water breaking
- Lower back pain

General Advice / Self-care:
- Stay calm and follow medical advice.
- Pack a hospital bag in advance.
- Practice breathing and relaxation techniques.

Prevention Tips:
- Attend antenatal classes for preparation.
- Plan for a skilled birth attendant.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Heavy bleeding or severe pain.
- Signs of infection (fever, foul discharge).
- Prolonged labor without progress.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
