Overview:
Injury prevention focuses on reducing risks of accidents at home, work, or outdoors.

Common Causes:
- Road traffic accidents
- Falls
- Workplace accidents

General Advice / Self-care:
- Wear seatbelts and helmets consistently.
- Use protective gear in sports and work.
- Keep homes free of trip hazards.

Prevention Tips:
- Follow traffic and workplace safety rules.
- Educate communities about injury prevention.
- Supervise children in risky environments.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Severe bleeding or broken bones.
- Head injuries with loss of consciousness.
- Burns or electrical injuries.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
