Overview:
Emergency care provides immediate medical attention for life-threatening conditions.

Common Scenarios:
- Heart attack or stroke
- Severe injuries or burns
- Breathing difficulties

General Advice / Self-care:
- Call emergency services immediately.
- Provide CPR if trained and necessary.
- Control severe bleeding with direct pressure.

Prevention Tips:
- Learn first aid and CPR skills.
- Keep emergency contacts accessible.
- Maintain a stocked first aid kit.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Any situation that is life-threatening or worsening rapidly.
- Loss of consciousness, severe chest pain, or breathing difficulty.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
