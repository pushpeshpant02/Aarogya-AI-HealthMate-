Overview:
Newborn care ensures the survival and healthy growth of infants.

Common Needs:
- Warmth and protection
- Frequent feeding (breastfeeding preferred)
- Hygiene and safe sleep

General Advice / Self-care:
- Breastfeed exclusively for the first 6 months.
- Keep the baby warm and clothed appropriately.
- Ensure clean surroundings to prevent infection.

Prevention Tips:
- Follow vaccination schedules.
- Practice safe sleeping positions (on the back).

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Difficulty breathing or persistent crying.
- Poor feeding or refusal to feed.
- Fever or signs of infection.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
