Overview:
Vaccination protects individuals and communities from infectious diseases.

Common Benefits:
- Reduces severity of illness
- Prevents spread of diseases
- Protects vulnerable populations

General Advice / Self-care:
- Follow national immunization schedules.
- Keep vaccination records updated.
- Encourage family members to get vaccinated.

Prevention Tips:
- Get seasonal flu shots annually.
- Ensure childhood vaccines are given on time.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Severe allergic reaction after a vaccine (rare).
- Persistent high fever or unusual symptoms post-vaccination.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
