Overview:
Asthma is a chronic respiratory disease that inflames and narrows the airways.

Common Symptoms:
- Wheezing
- Shortness of breath
- Chest tightness
- Persistent coughing (especially at night)

General Advice / Self-care:
- Use prescribed inhalers correctly.
- Avoid triggers like smoke, dust, and pollen.
- Track symptoms with an action plan.

Prevention Tips:
- Get flu and pneumonia vaccines.
- Maintain good air quality indoors.
- Reduce exposure to allergens.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Severe breathing difficulty not relieved by inhaler.
- Trouble speaking in full sentences.
- Blue lips or nails.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
