Overview:
Occupational health ensures the safety and well-being of workers in the workplace.

Common Issues:
- Back pain and repetitive strain injuries
- Exposure to chemicals or dust
- Stress and burnout

General Advice / Self-care:
- Use ergonomic furniture and posture correction.
- Take regular breaks during work.
- Wear protective equipment when needed.

Prevention Tips:
- Implement workplace safety policies.
- Conduct regular health and safety training.
- Promote work-life balance.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Persistent pain from work activities.
- Exposure to toxic substances.
- Mental health issues due to work stress.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
