Overview:
Influenza (flu) is a contagious respiratory illness caused by influenza viruses.

Common Symptoms:
- Fever or feeling feverish
- Cough and sore throat
- Runny or stuffy nose
- Body aches and fatigue
- Sometimes vomiting and diarrhea (more common in children)

General Advice / Self-care:
- Rest and drink plenty of fluids.
- Take paracetamol or ibuprofen for fever and aches.
- Stay home to avoid infecting others.

Prevention Tips:
- Get annual flu vaccination.
- Wash hands often and cover coughs/sneezes.
- Avoid close contact with sick individuals.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Difficulty breathing or chest pain.
- Severe weakness or confusion.
- Persistent high fever not improving.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
