Overview:
Certain symptoms are considered medical emergencies requiring immediate action.

Common Red Flags:
- Chest pain or pressure
- Severe shortness of breath
- Unconsciousness
- Seizures
- Suicidal thoughts

General Advice / Self-care:
- Do not delay; seek immediate emergency care.
- Call emergency services or use SOS facilities.

Prevention Tips:
- Educate about warning signs.
- Manage chronic conditions effectively.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- At the first sign of any red-flag symptoms.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
