Overview:
Nutrition is essential for health, growth, and disease prevention.

Common Symptoms of Poor Nutrition:
- Fatigue and weakness
- Weight loss or gain
- Increased susceptibility to infections

General Advice / Self-care:
- Eat a variety of fruits, vegetables, grains, and proteins.
- Stay hydrated with safe water.
- Practice mindful eating and portion control.

Prevention Tips:
- Limit sugar, salt, and unhealthy fats.
- Avoid ultra-processed foods.
- Promote healthy eating habits in children.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Persistent fatigue or weakness.
- Unexplained weight changes.
- Severe nutritional deficiencies.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
