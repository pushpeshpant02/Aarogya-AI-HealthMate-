Overview:
Immunization is the process of protecting individuals from infectious diseases through vaccines.

Common Benefits:
- Reduces severity and spread of infectious diseases
- Protects vulnerable populations through herd immunity

General Advice / Self-care:
- Follow national immunization schedules for children and adults.
- Keep personal vaccination records updated.
- Encourage family and community immunization.

Prevention Tips:
- Annual flu vaccination.
- Travel vaccines for specific regions.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Severe allergic reactions post-vaccination (rare).
- Persistent fever or unusual symptoms after vaccination.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
