Overview:
Obesity is a condition involving excessive body fat that increases the risk of chronic diseases.

Common Symptoms:
- Excess body weight and fat accumulation
- Breathlessness, fatigue, joint pain
- Snoring or sleep disturbances

General Advice / Self-care:
- Adopt a balanced diet with controlled portions.
- Engage in regular physical activity.
- Track progress with realistic goals.

Prevention Tips:
- Promote healthy eating in children.
- Limit high-calorie, processed foods.
- Stay active daily.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- BMI >30 with health complications.
- Difficulty losing weight despite lifestyle changes.
- Conditions like diabetes, heart disease, or sleep apnea.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
