Overview:
First aid refers to immediate assistance given to a sick or injured person before professional help arrives.

Common Scenarios:
- Cuts and wounds
- Burns
- Choking
- Fractures

General Advice / Self-care:
- Clean wounds with clean water and cover with sterile bandage.
- For burns, cool under running water (not ice).
- For choking, perform abdominal thrusts if trained.

Prevention Tips:
- Keep a first aid kit at home and work.
- Learn basic first aid and CPR techniques.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Severe bleeding, deep wounds, or broken bones.
- Burns covering large areas.
- Unconsciousness or difficulty breathing.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
