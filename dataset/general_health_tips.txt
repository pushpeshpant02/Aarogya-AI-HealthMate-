Overview:
General health tips help maintain overall well-being.

Common Principles:
- Balanced diet and regular exercise
- Adequate sleep and hydration
- Good hygiene

General Advice / Self-care:
- Wash hands regularly.
- Get recommended vaccinations.
- Avoid smoking and limit alcohol.

Prevention Tips:
- Routine health check-ups.
- Promote awareness about lifestyle diseases.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Persistent unexplained symptoms.
- Severe fatigue or weight changes.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
