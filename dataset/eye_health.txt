Overview:
Eye health involves maintaining vision and preventing eye diseases.

Common Symptoms (issues):
- Blurred vision
- Eye pain or redness
- Sensitivity to light

General Advice / Self-care:
- Take regular breaks from screens.
- Wear sunglasses in bright sunlight.
- Maintain good hygiene with contact lenses.

Prevention Tips:
- Have regular eye exams.
- Manage chronic conditions like diabetes and hypertension.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Sudden loss of vision.
- Severe eye pain or injury.
- Persistent redness or discharge.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
