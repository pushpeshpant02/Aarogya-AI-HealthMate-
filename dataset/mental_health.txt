Overview:
Mental health is a key aspect of overall well-being, affecting emotions, thoughts, and behavior.

Common Symptoms (issues):
- Persistent sadness or hopelessness
- Excessive worry or anxiety
- Withdrawal from social activities
- Trouble sleeping or concentrating

General Advice / Self-care:
- Stay socially connected.
- Exercise regularly.
- Use stress management techniques like meditation.
- Maintain good sleep habits.

Prevention Tips:
- Keep balance between work and rest.
- Avoid alcohol and drugs as coping mechanisms.

When to Seek Medical Help:
- Thoughts of self-harm or suicide.
- Prolonged inability to function daily.
- Severe or persistent mood disturbances.

Disclaimer: This is general advice, not a medical diagnosis.
Source: WHO / CDC / NHS
